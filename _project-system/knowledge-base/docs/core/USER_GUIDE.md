# User Guide: Master Plan

Welcome to the **Master Plan** high-performance training system. This application is designed for precision, focus, and athletic reliability.

---

## 1. Getting Started

### **Cloud Account**
1.  Sign in via the **Login Portal** to secure your data.
2.  **Sync:** All your logs and programs are automatically backed up to the cloud.

---

## 2. Navigation & Agenda

The home screen is your **Master Agenda**.

*   **Timeline:** A chronological view of your training history and upcoming schedule.
*   **Today's Objective:** The system automatically highlights the next prescribed session in your program.
*   **Vault:** Access your performance records, statistics, and momentum metrics.

---

## 3. The Program Library

Tap the **Dumbbell Icon** (Top Right) to access the **Program Library**. This is your repository of structured microcycles and training modules.

*   **Program Day:** View the sequence of days in your active program (e.g., "Day 1", "Day 2").
*   **Recommended:** The system marks the next prescribed day with a `REC` badge.
*   **Start:** Tap any day to preview the exercises, then tap **START** to initialize the session.

### **Active Session Card**
If you navigate away from a running session, an **Active Session Card** will appear at the top of the Library. Tap it to return to the Logger instantly.

### **Retroactive Logging**
If you need to log a session for a previous date:
1.  Tap the **+** (Plus) button on the Master Agenda.
2.  Select the session you performed.
3.  Complete the log as normal.

---

## 4. The Session Logger

Once a session is active, you enter **Focus Mode**. The app acts as an intelligent assistant to guide you through the workout.

### **The "Following Shadow" Engine**
- **Intelligent Seeking:** When a set is finished, the app automatically scans for and focuses on the **next incomplete task**.
- **Accordion Handoff:** Only one training block is expanded at a time to minimize distraction.
- **Auto-Advance:** For timed blocks, the system moves to the next item automatically when the timer completes.

### **Visual Language (States)**
- **Upcoming (Gray):** Tasks yet to be performed.
- **Focused (Orange):** The current active task. If you move away from the recommended set, the target will "breathe" orange to call for attention.
- **Complete (Green):** Tasks where all prescribed sets are logged.

### **Logging Performance**
- **Inputs:** Enter your actual Weight and Reps (or Time/Distance depending on the exercise).
- **Save:** Tap the checkmark to record the set. Progress bars will update instantly.
- **Finalize:** Tap **"END ACTIVITY"** once all blocks are complete to sync your performance to the Master Agenda.
